Our mission

Making all people share their skills and competencies with others in order to build a better society based on mutual help, good relationships and happiness.

Our vision

We know that all individuals are gifted with great competencies, skills and know-hows. We want everyone to realize it and become willing to share it with others, especially those in difficulties. By making the access to volunteer activities easier both for individuals and companies, we foresee a future world where citizen engagement will be the norm.
